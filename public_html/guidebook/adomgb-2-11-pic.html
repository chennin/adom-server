The entire level is filled with traps, except for the most direct route to 
the Assassin Prince's chamber.  It will be very obvious if the PC strays 
from this path.

The monsters are:

@ - The Assassin Prince
@ - assassins and master assassins
@ - doppleganger lords

Also found randomly on the level are: 

@ - dopplegangers
X - gray slayers
X - holy slayers
@ - muggers

The #s are secret doors.
